SEBI GUIDELINES FOR TECHNOLOGY COMPANIES AND FINTECH - INDIA

KEY PROVISIONS FOR M&A DUE DILIGENCE:

1. ALGORITHMIC TRADING
- All algo trading systems must be approved by SEBI
- Mandatory audit trail for all automated trades
- Risk management framework required
- Penalty for unauthorised algo trading: INR 25 crore

2. INVESTMENT ADVISOR REGISTRATION
- Companies providing investment advice must register with SEBI
- Qualification requirements for investment advisors
- Compliance officer mandatory
- Net worth requirement: INR 50 lakhs (individuals), INR 150 lakhs (corporates)

3. DATA LOCALISATION FOR MARKET DATA
- All market data of Indian investors must be stored in India
- Real-time data sharing with foreign entities requires SEBI approval

4. ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING
- KYC mandatory for all investment products
- Suspicious transaction reporting to FIU-IND
- Record keeping for 5 years minimum

5. M&A CHECKLIST
- Is target registered as Investment Advisor with SEBI? YES/NO
- Any SEBI enforcement actions pending? YES/NO
- AML/KYC processes in place? YES/NO
- Algo systems approved by exchange? YES/NO

6. PENALTIES
- Operating without registration: INR 1 crore per day
- AML violations: INR 25 crore or 3x the profit
